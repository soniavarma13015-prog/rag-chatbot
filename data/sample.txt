Acme Corporation — Employee Leave Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per 
calendar year. Leave accrues at a rate of 1.67 days per month. Unused leave 
may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the following year. Leave 
must be approved by the employee's line manager at least 5 business days in 
advance.

2. Sick Leave
Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical 
certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Sick leave 
does not carry over to the following year.

3. Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave 
following the birth or adoption of a child. Secondary caregivers receive 4 
weeks of paid leave. Leave must commence within 6 months of the birth or 
adoption date.

4. Public Holidays
Employees observe all nationally recognised public holidays. If a public 
holiday falls on a weekend, the following Monday is treated as the substitute 
holiday.

5. Unpaid Leave
Employees may request up to 30 days of unpaid leave per year for personal 
reasons, subject to management approval.
